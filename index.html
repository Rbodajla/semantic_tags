Canada to the United States

I grew up in a small city called Fredericton, New Brunswick Canada. Where I loved playing High School sports which included Soccer, Basketball and Volleyball. It was my childhood dream to play College Basketball in the United States. In 2011 my family and I moved to Norwood, Massachusetts for my parents career and not my basketball career.

Priorities:Sports vs. Educations

I completed High School and College in Massachusetts and had the opportunity to play Division III Basketball and Lacrosse. Education became my primary focus and obtaining my Master's Degree in Global Supply Chain Management at Maine Maritime Academybecame my number one focus.

Career Trials and Tribulations

After graduating I decided to move to Long Beach, California and purse my career as a Longshoreman Superintendent. I quickly realized this wasn't my long term career. I switched my focus to Warehouse Management and then Leadership Development for Kraft Foods and Amazon. I had the wonderful opportunity to work and live all over the United States.Connecticut, Nevada, Tennessee, Indiana, California, Texas and Washington. I have lived in Seattle for the past 2 years and now consider the Pacific Northwest Home.

Life Isn't Just About Work

In my spare time I enjoy traveling around the world, exploring the Pacific Northwest, Downhill skiing/touring, and the most important thing... spending time with my family, friends and cats.
